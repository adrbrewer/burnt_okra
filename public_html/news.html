Burnt Okra News

Burnt Okra Rocks Cedar County Fair

Recently the band made a trip to Cedar County Iowa to perform for a county fair. Thousands of Burnt Okra fans were waiting for them there. The fair director said it was the best turnout of any other fair performance. Burnt Okra is the perfect band for your county fair!

Burnt Okra's very own drummer Jim Johnson welcomes a daughter to his family

Last Tuesday, Jim Johnson welcomed his baby girl Ashley into the world at 2:30 AM. She came as a healthy baby weighing 6 lbs 5 oz. Say congratulations to Jim if you see him around!

Burnt Okra performs at Kinnick Stadium

With the college football season under way, Burnt Okra gave its annual charity concert at Kinnick Stadium for the children's hospital in Iowa City. The crowds keep growing with every year and so do the donations. Thank you to all of the Burnt Okra fans who came to the concert.

Burnt Okra is a rock band from Des Moines Iowa, we have played all over Iowa, Nebraska, and in Missouri. We have a passion for the music that we play and love sharing that music with others. On this website you will be able to find out more about our band members, purchase merchandise, find our social media accounts, and find our upcoming shows.
